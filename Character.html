Know More About Characters

Frieren

Over 1,000 years old | Mage | Elf

Frieren is the oldest character in both parties. She is an elf who has lived well past 1,000 years and still isn't considered old by her species. Because she has lived so long, Frieren has learned a lot about magic, making her an incredibly powerful mage. Magic users typically give off manna, so Frieren hides her true manna powers to mislead enemies.

Himmel

Dead in 76 years old | Hero | Human

Himmel is touted as one of the greatest heroes of all time. He was the de facto leader of the party that defeated the Demon King, and he was always ready to help those in need. When their party returned from their ten-year journey, Himmel was twenty-six. Yet, when Frieren visits him again decades later, he is seventy-six. He dies shortly after.

Fern

18 years old | Mage | Human

By the time Fern and Frieren begin their journey, Fern is in her late teens. She is a human who was raised by Heiter and taken in by Frieren after his passing. Fern was still a child when she met Frieren.Although Fern appears to be an average human female physically, she is truly one of the best, most powerful mages around. Even her simplest spells can have a great impact. Fern is a rising star in the world of Frieren thanks to her upbringing and her desire to prove herself as a capable individual.

Stark

18 years old | Warrior | Human

Like Fern, Stark is another exceptionally powerful human. He was born in a warrior village that valued fighting skills above all else, which predisposed Stark to one day becoming a great warrior. At only eighteen years old, Stark can easily take down enemies and carve great crevasses into the sides of mountains. Stark is very talented, but a lot of credit also goes to his master and guardian, Eisen, who took the boy in after Stark's village was destroyed by demons.
