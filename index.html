STORY

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Elf mage Frieren and her courageous fellow adventurers have defeated the Demon King and brought peace to the land. But Frieren will long outlive the rest of her former party. How will she come to understand what life means to the people around her?

Decades after their victory, the funeral of Himmel, one of her courageous, confronts Frieren with her own near immortality. Frieren sets out to fulfill the last wishes of her comrades and finds herself beginning a new adventure…

On this new adventure, new companions gradually join her. Each one carries a unique story and fate, as if the threads of destiny bind them to her. With every encounter, a new chapter begins, and her team grows stronger through their shared bond.
